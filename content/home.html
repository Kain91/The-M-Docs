The-M-Docs

This is the official documentation of The-M-Project.
It exists beside the official JSDoc documentation but will replace the wiki on GitHub.

You're invited to actively participate in extending our documentation. You can do this by forking our The-M-Docs repository on GitHub and then, after you finished your work, sending pull requests to us.

It's work in progress but grows from day to day. If you have any suggestions, send us some feedback via Twitter (@_themproject), iRC (#themproject on irc.freenode.net) or via our contact formular.
